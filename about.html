Welcome to check out my web development projects and hear my story:

As a mechanical engineer constantly working with physical structures, it was a unique journey for me to finally step into the cyber world. In 2019, I started to explore my interests in robotics and computer vision, where I inevitably encountered Python as the top choice for data analysis and machine learning. I had written code for an Arduino light tracing car, and for parsing and extracting data from thousands of excel sheets. However, it was the first time I started to actually learn a programing language, as well as data structures and algorithms.

I was intrigued and realized this is where I want to build my career. Instead of getting a computer science degree, I choose to start with web development and to learn it on my own during my free time for three reasons:

  • Grad school is merely a brief interlude on the learning journey, especially in programming.
  • There are lots of resource online that you can learn the same content as in school.
  • Grad school costs a lot.

The journey is ongoing.
