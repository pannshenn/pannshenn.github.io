What's this website about?

Simon Game generates a series of tones and lights for user to repeat the sequence in order to proceed to next level. Instead of using four colors traditionally, the Mamamoo variation uses the profiles of four members of a Korean girl group Mamamoo.

Play the Game

Click on the next button (the green one) to begin the game. If user succeeds and is ready to play the next level, click on the next button again to start the new level. To restart the game, simply click on the stop button (the red one).
